Paper Wallet for all Crypto Coins

This tool helps you to quickly create a nicely formatted paper wallet with auto-generated QR-codes.
  • Disconnect your computer from the internet
  • Generate your public and private addresses on another website or with another tool
  • Enter the name of the cryptocoin in the top-left field
  • Enter the public information in the left side.
    You can enter 1 or 2 public strings and specify the name of that key.
    Usually the title will be "Address" and you can enter the public address in the field below.
    A QR-code is auto-created.
  • Enter the private information on the right side.
    Enter the type of key name like: "Private Key" and the actual key below that.
    You can enter 3 different keys/secrets/etc.
  • Click the print link at the top-left to auto-remove unused fields and print on an A4/US letter in landscape.
  • Cut away the parts you don't need and fold the private key over the middle section
